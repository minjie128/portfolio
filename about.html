About Me.

My name is Jie Min (pronounced "Jee-eh Meen"). I like to design and code.

Currently I am a UX Designer at Expeditors, helping them build the Customs and Compliance system. I graduated from the University of Washington's Master of Science in Information Management (MSIM) program, where I studied user centered design, information architectue, user research, usability tests and data visualization.

As Don Norman said, “The new me is beauty.” I enjoy making things neat and fun as well as the wonderful process of generating projects from concepts to deliverables. I am always seeking opportunities to learn from other designers in the User Experience field and am working very hard to develop myself as a "full stack" designer in the future.

Besides design, I love

  • playing the violin
  • swimming and playing ping pong
  • participate in volunteer activities
  • traveling around the world

I like fun projects. I like fun people.
